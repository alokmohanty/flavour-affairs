Our Policies

Transparency in how we operate and serve you.

Privacy Policy

At Flavour Affairs, we are committed to protecting your privacy. We collect personal information (such as your name, email address, and phone number) solely for the purpose of communicating with you regarding your catering inquiries and bookings.

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. All data is stored securely and used exclusively to enhance your experience with our services.

Terms of Service

By booking with Flavour Affairs, you agree to our standard terms of service:

  • Quotations: All quotes are valid for 30 days from the date of issue.
  • Dietary Requirements: Final dietary requirements must be submitted at least 14 days prior to the event date. We cannot guarantee accommodations for requests made after this deadline.
  • Liability: Flavour Affairs holds comprehensive public liability insurance. However, we are not liable for any issues arising from food handling or storage once the food has been handed over to the client or consumed outside of our direct service times.

Cancellation & Refund Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair while covering costs incurred for your event preparations:

  • 30+ Days Notice: Full refund of deposit, minus a small administrative fee.
  • 14-29 Days Notice: 50% refund of the deposit.
  • Less than 14 Days Notice: No refund possible, as ingredients and staff have already been secured.

For any questions regarding these policies, please reach out via our Contact Page.
